Natural Language Processing Research

I spent Summer 2022 in New York, NY, working as a research assistant in Dr. Julia Hirschberg's Speech Lab at Columbia University. My funding came from the Barnard College Summer Research Institute.

My Project

My project focused on building a model to detect and generate empathetic human speech. Plenty of research has been done on empathetic text, but not much has been done on the acoustic-prosodic features which make speech sound empathetic.

The project was still in its early stages when I worked at the lab, so much of my work was on data collection and preprocessing.

At the end of the summer, I presented a poster at the 2022 Lida Orzeck '68 Poster Session. Check out my poster below!
